My Fav Basketball Player: Kyrie Irving.

PLAYER NEWS

Kyrie Irving said he's completely recovered from the right shoulder procedure that ended his 2019-20 campaign after just 20 games.

Irving's 2019-20 campaign was nothing short of disastrous in terms of games played, but he did look good in his limited floor time and will obviously be looking to bounce back in a major way next year as the Nets go on an official title chase with a healthy Kevin Durant. Irving is too risky to take in the first round on draft day, but if he's able to stay healthy, there's a good chance he'll return first-round value.

Kyrie Irving (right shoulder) will not travel with the Nets to Orlando.
